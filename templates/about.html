03 : About me

Why Coding?

I have always been fascinated by the world of technology and how it is constantly evolving. In grade 10, I stepped into the world of coding, it immediately became my interest. There is something incredibly satisfying about taking a problem, breaking it down into small pieces, and then using logic and creativity to solve it. Every time I write a piece of code that works, it feels like a victory. As I got older, I realized that this interest could be turned into a career, and I decided to pursue coding related career in the future.

In my opinion, coding is a way to make a tangible impact on the world. Whether it's developing an app that makes people's lives easier, creating a website that connects people across the globe, or writing software that powers critical systems, coding has the power to make a real difference.
